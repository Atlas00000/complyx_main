# Complyx - IFRS S1 & S2 Readiness Assessment Platform

## Project Overview

**Complyx** is an intelligent conversational platform designed to help organizations assess their readiness for IFRS S1 (Sustainability-related Financial Information Disclosures) and IFRS S2 (Climate-related Disclosures) compliance. The system uses advanced AI to conduct natural, adaptive conversations that evaluate an organization's sustainability compliance status and provide actionable guidance.

### What are IFRS S1 & S2?

IFRS S1 and S2 are international sustainability reporting standards that require organizations to disclose how sustainability and climate-related risks and opportunities affect their business strategy, governance, and financial performance. These standards are becoming mandatory for many organizations globally, requiring comprehensive assessment and documentation of sustainability practices.

## Use Cases

### Primary Use Cases

1. **Initial Compliance Assessment**
   - Organizations preparing for mandatory IFRS S1/S2 adoption
   - Baseline evaluation of current sustainability practices
   - Identification of compliance gaps before formal reporting

2. **Ongoing Readiness Monitoring**
   - Regular check-ins to track progress toward compliance
   - Monitoring improvements in sustainability practices
   - Staying current with evolving requirements

3. **Pre-Audit Preparation**
   - Self-assessment before external audits
   - Documentation of compliance efforts
   - Evidence gathering for regulatory reviews

4. **Cross-Functional Team Training**
   - Educating teams on IFRS requirements
   - Building internal capacity for sustainability reporting
   - Standardizing understanding across departments

5. **Consultant & Advisor Support**
   - Advisory firms assisting clients with compliance
   - Rapid assessment of client organizations
   - Standardized evaluation methodology

## Technical Foundation

### Core Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: Modern web application built with Next.js, providing fast, responsive user experience
- **Backend**: Node.js server infrastructure ensuring reliable, scalable performance
- **Database**: PostgreSQL for secure, persistent data storage
- **AI Engine**: Google Gemini AI for natural language processing and intelligent conversations
- **Knowledge Base**: Semantic search capabilities enabling context-aware responses from IFRS documentation

### Platform Capabilities

- **Multi-User Support**: Multiple team members can collaborate on assessments
- **Session Persistence**: All conversations and assessments are saved automatically
- **Data Security**: Enterprise-grade security measures protecting organizational information
- **Mobile Responsive**: Full functionality on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Cloud-Ready**: Scalable architecture supporting growth from small teams to enterprise deployments
